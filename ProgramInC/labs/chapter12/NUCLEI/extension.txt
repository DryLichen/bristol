1. comment: 
    Comment can be put in a line alone or at the end of a statement.

2. tolerance of nonstandard literal lisp
    Even the number of spaces is not standard in a literal lisp, interpreter will still
    interpret it correctly.

3. New Grammar

    # (IF (CONDITION) ((IF-TRUE-INSTRCTS)) ELIF (CONDITION) ((IF-TRUE-INSTRCTS)) ((IF_FALSE_INSTRCTS)) )
    <IF>       ::= "IF" "(" <BOOLFUNC> ")" "(" <INSTRCTS> "(" <INSTRCTS> 
                   | "IF" "(" <BOOLFUNC> ")" "(" <INSTRCTS> <ELIFS> <INSTRCTS>

    <ELIFS>    ::= <ELSE> <ELIFS> | "("
    <ELIF>     ::= "ELIF" "(" <BOOLFUNC> ")" "(" <INSTRCTS>
